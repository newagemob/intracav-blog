A comprehensive oncology guideline library synthesizing recommendations from multiple professional societies into copyright-free, clinically dependable references. Each super-guideline integrates evidence from 3–6 authoritative sources, presenting unified recommendations with full source citations.

---

## Vascular Access & Infusion Safety

| Guideline | Scope |
|---|---|
| [Central Venous Access Devices in Oncology](/oncology/guidelines/central-venous-access/) | Device selection, insertion, maintenance, complication management, and quality metrics for CVADs in cancer patients |
| [Chemotherapy Extravasation Prevention & Management](/oncology/guidelines/chemotherapy-extravasation/) | Agent classification (vesicant/irritant/non-vesicant), prevention strategies, antidote protocols, and follow-up care |
| [Cancer-Associated Thrombosis & CVAD-Related VTE](/oncology/guidelines/cancer-associated-thrombosis/) | Risk assessment (Khorana, Vienna CATS), prophylaxis, DOAC/LMWH treatment, and catheter-related thrombosis |
