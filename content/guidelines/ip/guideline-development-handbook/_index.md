A comprehensive methodology handbook for developing clinical practice guidelines, expert guidance documents, consensus statements, and practice statements in infection prevention and control (IPC), healthcare epidemiology, and antibiotic stewardship.

This handbook provides a standardized, rigorous framework used by guideline committees to ensure consistency, transparency, and scientific integrity throughout the document development lifecycle. It covers the complete process from initial topic proposal through publication, including systematic literature search and review procedures, evidence classification systems, consensus-building methods, writing panel composition, conflict of interest management, and detailed development timelines.

Originally adapted from established professional society methodology and updated through October 2024, this resource serves as a reference for any organization developing evidence-based infection prevention recommendations.[^1]
